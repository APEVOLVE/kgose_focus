Booking Policy

A non-refundable 50% of the balance due is required to confirm and secure the booking.
Bookings must be done at least 7 days before the day of production.
Any bookings done less than 7 days before the day of production will have an additional cost of 5% of the balance due.

Cancelation Policy

Any booking cancelation forfeits the 50% booking deposit

Postponing Policy

Based on natural causes (bad climate circumstances), the day of production will be postponed at no additional costs.
Postponing the day of production based on other circumstances other than natural causes, there will be an additional of 30% of the balance due.
